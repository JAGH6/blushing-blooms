What is Tetris?

Tetris is a tile-matching puzzle game where falling pieces (tetrominoes) must be rotated and moved to form solid horizontal lines. Clear lines to score points and keep the stack from reaching the top. Simple to learn, hard to master.

Family competition

Play once per day. Sign in with email (we send a free code — no password). Your best score that day is saved. We keep a daily leaderboard, streaks, achievements, and a family journal (daily notes). Optional: daily text to the family with the winner. Check the stats page for your high score, streaks, and most daily wins.

How to play

Use the keyboard to move and rotate pieces. Clear lines to advance through 10 levels (each level speeds up). No points for soft drop — score comes from line clears, level bonuses, and speed if you beat the game. Answer the daily trivia (6 multiple-choice options) after your run for a chance at +100 bonus points.
