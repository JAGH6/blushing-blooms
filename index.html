Tetris

The classic block-stacking puzzle game

What is Tetris?

Tetris is a tile-matching puzzle game where falling pieces (tetrominoes) must be rotated and moved to form solid horizontal lines. Clear lines to score points and keep the stack from reaching the top. Simple to learn, hard to master.

How to play

Use the keyboard to move and rotate pieces. Clear complete lines to earn points and make room for more pieces.

  • ← Move left
  • → Move right
  • ↓ Soft drop (move down faster)
  • ↑ Rotate piece
